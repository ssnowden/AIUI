Templates - Platonic Organisms, Organisms and Molecules

Use AIUI's 'templates' best practice to build a page's html.

Best practice here is to use the Atomic Design principles. This is based on the idea of atoms at the bottom of the template hiearchy consisting of the html, css and various js libraries that we use. Next are the molecules, bringing together a number of atoms (html tags, css classes, scripts, etc.) to create a single display item such as a submit button.

At the top are the organisms formed by bringing together a range of molecules to create an interface - in this case a web page. Here we will look at the Django templates that move from molecular level to the complete organism. This will focus primarily at the molecular level but will also include some organism examples to demonstrate how this is all brought together.

We will start with some of the larger molecules (probably better described as platonic organisms taken from the notion of Platonic ideal forms) and work our way down to smaller and smaller molecules.
